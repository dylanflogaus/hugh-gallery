About the Artist

Painting the soft edges of the world

"I want people to feel like they've stepped into a garden after rain — that sense of stillness and colour."

Hugh Phibbs is a contemporary artist based in the Pacific Northwest, known for luminous watercolour works that blur the line between abstraction and the natural world.
